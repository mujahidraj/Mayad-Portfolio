Heart Disease Quantum Prediction: A Machine Learning Approach

M

Asmaul Husna Mayad

Researcher & Data Analyst

Abstract

This research presents a robust machine learning model designed to analyze complex health datasets for the early prediction of heart disease. By implementing advanced **Preprocessing** techniques and **Classification Algorithms**, the model identifies risk factors with high accuracy. The study aims to bridge the gap between raw medical data and actionable diagnostic insights .

Key Objectives

  • To clean and preprocess noisy medical datasets.
  • To implement and compare multiple classification algorithms.
  • To visualize risk factors for better clinical decision-making.
